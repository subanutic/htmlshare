Citadel of Hopeful Heaven

This Citadel consists of multiple dance clips, 1 optional wallhop, very punishing gameplay, a quiz, 13 stud long jumps, and more propaganda to find out yourselves. This Citadel was made using random stuff from the toolbox and not the Official EToH Tower Building Kit which Disclaimer: This Citadel is made with extremely poor quality in which it also does not have No Clip Tool. This Citadel carefully made by Subnautic & GreatWhite64567 is one of the most beautiful creations ever. CoHH is presently unverified and has no real winners yet.
